About Me

In 2015, at DCC I double-majored in Computer Information Systems(CIS) and General Science(LAX) and was accepted to State University of NY, New Paltz with more than 80 credits, having never attended due to family related issues.

In 2016 I learned python and wanted to put these skills to the test and started to build Django projects from several blogs and books. Django stuck with me because of the comprehensive documentation. Now programming in Django and Python is my main career focus, to become an expert in both.
